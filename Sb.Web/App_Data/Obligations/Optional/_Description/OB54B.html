Pay as You Go (PAYG) instalments is a system for making regular payments towards your expected tax bill to save you from a large tax bill at the end of the year.

You can register for PAYG voluntarily, however the ATO will let you know when you need to pay by instalments based on your income tax return.

Alternatively, you might choose to put money away regularly in a bank account to cover a possible tax bill.
