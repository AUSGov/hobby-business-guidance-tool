A business name is a title under which a person, or other legal entity, trades. You must register a business name if you want to trade under a name other than your own.

Before you register a business name, you should check that:

  • the business name is available
  • the domain name is available if you intend to have a website
  • the name is available as a trade mark.

You’ll need an Australian Business Number (ABN) before you register your business name and you can apply for both at the same time.
