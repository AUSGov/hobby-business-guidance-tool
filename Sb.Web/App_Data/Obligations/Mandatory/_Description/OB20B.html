When you’re in business, you will need to declare your income to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) in your annual return. Check the tax return for guidance on where to declare this income.

Find out more about what income you need to include.

You will need to report the amounts you earn from your business if you:

  • receive certain payments, such as from Centrelink or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA).
  • make payments where income is considered, such as for child support.

Read more about income reporting on the Department of Human Services website.

Read more about notifying DVA on the Department of Veterans’ Affairs website.

If you rely on this tool for tax purposes, the ATO’s commitment statement applies to you. Make sure you keep a copy of the outcome you get under the tool.
