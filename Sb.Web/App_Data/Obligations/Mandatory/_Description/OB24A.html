To get an answer on your specific circumstances, you can:

  • talk to the ATO Business line on 13 28 66, who can help you decide if you are a hobby or business
  • you can request an early engagement discussion with the ATO for private advice about your circumstances. They can also talk to you about whether you need a private ruling from the ATO, which will go through your answers to these questions in more detail and provide an answer that protects you from tax shortfall, penalties and interest
  • talk to a business adviser in your area who can help you decide using the results of this tool
  • use your answers to start a conversation with an accountant or legal adviser
