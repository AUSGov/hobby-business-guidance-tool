If you’re a hobby, you do not need to declare the amounts you make from your hobby to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO). You cannot claim deductions for expenses you incur for your hobby either.

If you’re in business, you will need to declare your income to the ATO in your annual return. Check the tax return for guidance on where to declare this income.

Find out more about what income you can exclude and income you need to include.

You will need to report the amounts you earn from your hobby or business if you:

  • receive certain payments, such as from Centrelink or the Department of Veterans’ Affairs (DVA).
  • make payments where income is considered, such as for child support.

Read more about income reporting on the Department of Human Services website.

Read more about notifying DVA on the Department of Veterans’ Affairs website.
